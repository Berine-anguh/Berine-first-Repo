MY FAVOURITE MEAL IS ACHU AND YELLOW SOUP

A) Achu

Achu is the traditional meal of a number of villages in the North West region of Cameroon which includes my village AWING too. This meal can be prepared in so many occasions, furthermore could equally be prepared to pay a visit. We the Awingâ€™s mostly have varieties of cocoyam used in the preparation. Few of this below could be used; White cocoyam, red cocoyam, etc. Below are the steps used in preparing Achu.

  • The dug cocoyam from the farmland is been washed,
  • Then later on place it on fire after the washing,
  • Mark it above the cocoyam in the pot with clean/distilled water,
  • Allow it to get boil for 45 mins.
  • Now getting to the end point, banana is been added to the cocoyam.

The addition of the banana at that time is to make it get ready at the same time with the Achu, but in case the cocoyam gets ready when the banana was not put on time, the banana will need a proper boiling on its own. After all is ready, requirements like mortar, pistle, etc for the pounding of the Achu.
