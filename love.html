These books have been structured in to units, following the eight integrated learning themes with familiar examples to facilitate the learner’s understanding. Each unit constitute an integrated learning theme and each theme takes into consideration the learner’s environment.

Learning outcomes have been stated to facilitate the work of the teachers and parents when working with the learners, to enable learners to be actively involved in learning activities. Evaluation

Evaluation activities have been included and a lot of these activities are thought provoking thus learners have been given the latitude to think and come out with their own activity hence auto-evaluate themselves.

ANUCAN Series are all in line with the new curricula of Cameroon Nursery Education. They can be easily exploited by the teacher and even parents who know little or nothing about the Nursery School.

ACTIVITIES

A variety of activities have been provided and group work has been encouraged. This will make learners to be actively involve involved during the learning, and teachers can also evaluate the learner’s attitude easily when they are working in groups
